F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is SketchApp?

    SketchApp is a collaborative online drawing tool that lets you draw with your friends.

  • How to open a new canvas?

    Go to the Homepage and click on start drawing.

  • How to start drawing?

    Once you've opened a drawing, click the left mouse button and moving it around lets your draw your desired drawing.

  • How to change colors?

    Click the burger icon on the left. This will pull in the side tool bar. There are three sliders for Red Green Blue respectively. You could mix in the RGB values to get the desired color.

  • How to make my drawing thicker?

    Click the burger icon on the left. This will pull in the side tool bar. In the thickness section there is a slider that lets you change the thickness left being low and right being higher.

  • Can I save my drawing?

    Yes, you can! Click the burger icon on the left. This will pull in the side tool bar. In the download section, click on download. This saves the image to your computer.

  • Does my friends work save as well?

    Yes, it does. SketchApp is a collaborative app and it lets you save the collaboration of your work.

  • I've made a mistake. How do I erase it?

    Click the burger icon on the left. This will pull in the side tool bar. You can change the color to white. This will let you erase your drawing.
